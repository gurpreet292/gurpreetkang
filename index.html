This program offers a 3-year residential BCA course in collaboration with Eternal University, NavGurukul, and Shri Badrika Ashram.The 3-year BCA program by NavGurukul, in collaboration with Eternal University and Shri Badrika Ashram, represents a significant effort to democratize education and provide life-changing opportunities to underprivileged girls. Through this initiative, these young women are not only gaining academic knowledge but are also being prepared to take on the challenges of the modern workplace, ultimately leading to a more equitable society.

Program Details

Information about the BCA program, curriculum, residential facilities.3-Year BCA Program Overview The BCA program is a comprehensive 3-year residential course designed to equip students with in-depth knowledge and practical skills in computer applications, preparing them for careers in the tech industry. Curriculum: The curriculum is designed to cover all essential areas of computer applications, including programming, software development, database management, web technologies, and more. It also includes soft skills training, communication skills, and personality development. Eligibility: The program is specifically targeted at underprivileged girls who have completed their 12th standard (or equivalent) and have a keen interest in pursuing a career in technology. Funding: The program is fully funded, covering tuition, accommodation, meals, and other essential expenses. This ensures that financial constraints do not hinder the students' ability to pursue their education. Residential Facilities: Students are provided with residential facilities, creating a supportive community where they can focus on their studies without worrying about external distractions. Job Placement: The program includes a strong focus on employability, with the final year dedicated to internships, projects, and placement assistance. NavGurukul has a network of corporate partners that helps in securing jobs for the graduates.

Application

How to apply, eligibility criteria.Application Process How to Apply: Interested candidates can apply through the NavGurukul website or other designated platforms. The selection process typically involves an application form, an aptitude test, and a personal interview. Selection Criteria: The selection process is designed to identify candidates who have the potential to succeed in the program and are committed to pursuing a career in technology. **6. **Impact and Outcomes Empowerment: The program is more than just an educational course; it's a means of empowerment. By providing these young women with the skills and confidence to enter the tech industry, the program aims to break the cycle of poverty and enable them to lead independent and successful lives. Community Building: Graduates of the program often become role models in their communities, inspiring other girls to pursue education and professional careers.

About Us

The program is a collaborative effort by NavGurukul, Eternal University, and Shri Badrika Ashram, aiming to uplift marginalized communities through quality education.

NavGurukul

NavGurukul is a non-profit organization focused on providing affordable, high-quality education to underserved communities, particularly women.

Eternal University

Eternal University offers a range of academic programs with a focus on holistic education, blending academic rigor with spiritual and ethical development.

Shri Badrika Ashram

Shri Badrika Ashram is dedicated to social welfare and education, supporting initiatives that empower the underprivileged through quality education.

Program Details

The BCA program covers a wide range of subjects in computer applications, including programming, software development, database management, and web technologies.

Curriculum Overview

The curriculum is designed to provide in-depth knowledge and practical skills in computer applications, preparing students for successful careers in the tech industry.

Residential Facilities

The program offers full residential facilities, ensuring that students can focus on their studies without worrying about external distractions.

Job Placement
